Curriculum Vitae

Professor of Statistics and Data Science and top-10 researcher with an interdisciplinary profile and several publications in outstanding scientific journals (rated as top 5% scientists with their first publication in 2016 on ResearchGate). Supervised 20+ B.Sc. and M.Sc. theses, 7 Ph.D. dissertations (5 ongoing, 2 complete) with strong philosophy of teaching applied statistics. In my young academic career since 2016, when I did my Ph.D. in statistics, I received research grants of over 1,000,000 euros. I am also actively involved in the scientific community as associate editor of 3 journals and treasurer of the German Statistical Society.

In my main research area (topic T.6863 "Spillover Effect; Panel Study; Autoregressive Model"), I am currently ranked 4th worldwide on Scopus and 1st among UK researchers.
